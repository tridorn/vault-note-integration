## Ⅲ · PROMOTION & CULLING ALGORITHM (*Up-or-Out*)  

**Tri-Axis Score (0-100 each)**  
```
 S₁  Mission Outcome Index  (weight = 0.45)
 S₂  Doctrine Contribution  (weight = 0.30)
 S₃  Leadership Effect Q.   (weight = 0.25)
```
```
TotalScore = Σ(Sᵢ × weightᵢ)
```
- **≥ 80** → promotable  
- **50-79** → stagnation grace 12 mo  
- **< 50** → remediation or exit  

Scores feed real-time from Notion dashboards (mission KR completion, AAR adoption count, Pulse-NPS).

## Ⅳ · SHADOW-BOARD OF YOUNG OFFICERS (SBYO) — THE MERITOCRATIC GYROSCOPE

| Parameter          | Spec                                           |
|--------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| **Cohort Size**    | 5 % of total Officer Corps (rounded-up) |
| **Eligibility**    | O-0–O-2 scoring ≥ 85 in last bi-annual review + IG clearance |
| **Tenure**         | 12 months, non-renewable |
| **Mandate**        | Parallel deliberation on all LC dockets; submit *Dissenting Memorandum* within 24 h |
| **Powers**         | Recommend pause, pivot, or risk escalation; LC must issue formal answer within 7 days (*Sunlight Rule*) |
| **Protection**     | IG ombudsman; retaliation = instant IG court-martial |
| **Outputs**        | % adoption tracked; integrated into promotion dossiers—high-quality dissent = brownie points |

> *Culturally, SBYO is the “anti-entropy inoculation” preventing senior ossification.*

## Ⅴ · PIPELINE SYNTHESIS—HOW TALENT FLOWS  

1. **Recruit** → EMCA → O-0  
2. **Dual AMU rotations** (probation)  
3. **Field Board** → O-1 (LFO)  
4. **Task-Force command OR CEP pilot**  
   *  • Task-Force success → O-2*  
   *  • CEP venture success → Innovator track (seed) / lateral Officer O-2*  
5. **SCAWV** → O-3 Staff Officer  
6. **AGS Cell chief OR Strategic-Command deputy** → O-4 / O-5  
7. **LC candidacy** (O-6), vetted by IG & JVFC track-record  
8. **Stagnation** at any grade > 3 y → *out-rotation* to Specialist or external alumni network.
