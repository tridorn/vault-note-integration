## Definition

Margin Level[^1]Â is the ratio between [[Equity]] and [[Used Margin]]. It is expressed as a percentage. For example, if your Equity is $5,000 and the Used Margin is $1,000, the Margin Level is 500%.[^2]

## General

### Also called

- Margin Indicator

### How to calculate

Margin Level = (Equity / Used Margin) x 100%
